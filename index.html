About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Electrical, Computer, and Biomedical Engineering, University of Rhode Island, USA. My research interests lie in Machine Learning, Signal Processing, and Biomedical Data Analysis.

I recieved my Ph.D. in Machine Learning from Georgia Institute of Technology advised by Prof. Yongxin Chen. I received my Masters degee in Signal Processing from Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology, advised by Prof. B  S  Manoj.
